category: company
title: About NimbusDesk

NimbusDesk is a SaaS platform that helps businesses manage customer communication across email, live chat, social media, and web forms from a single workspace.

Founded in 2021, NimbusDesk serves small and medium-sized businesses, startups, and enterprise organizations across more than 30 countries.

The platform focuses on customer support automation, team collaboration, analytics, and AI-powered workflow optimization.

---

category: company
title: Mission and Vision

NimbusDesk's mission is to help support teams deliver faster and more personalized customer experiences through intelligent automation.

The company's vision is to become the central operating system for customer support teams by combining communication channels, AI capabilities, analytics, and workflow automation into a unified platform.

---

category: product
title: Shared Inbox

The Shared Inbox feature allows multiple support agents to manage customer conversations collaboratively.

Key capabilities include conversation assignment, internal notes, collision detection, tagging, SLA monitoring, and team mentions.

Shared Inbox supports email, live chat, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp Business, and web contact forms.

---

category: product
title: AI Ticket Classification

NimbusDesk uses machine learning models to automatically categorize incoming customer requests.

Supported categories include Billing, Technical Support, Account Management, Authentication, Feature Requests, Sales Inquiries, and General Questions.

Administrators can review classification confidence scores and manually adjust classifications when necessary.

---

category: product
title: AI Response Assistant

AI Response Assistant helps support agents draft customer responses using company knowledge and conversation history.

The assistant can summarize conversations, suggest replies, generate troubleshooting instructions, and recommend relevant knowledge base articles.

Generated responses always require agent review before being sent to customers.

---

category: product
title: Knowledge Base Management

NimbusDesk provides a built-in knowledge base system that allows organizations to create and publish help articles.

Knowledge base content can be organized into categories, collections, and searchable documentation portals.

AI features can use approved knowledge base content to assist support agents during customer interactions.

category: pricing
title: Subscription Plans

NimbusDesk offers Starter, Growth, and Enterprise plans.

Starter Plan costs $19 per user per month and includes Shared Inbox, Email Support, and Basic Analytics.

Growth Plan costs $49 per user per month and includes AI Ticket Classification, Live Chat, Workflow Automation, and Priority Support.

Enterprise Plan includes custom pricing, dedicated account management, advanced security controls, custom integrations, and 24/7 support.

title: Billing and Subscription Management

Customers can upgrade their subscription plans at any time.

Downgrades are applied at the beginning of the next billing cycle.

title: Security Overview

NimbusDesk follows industry-standard security practices to protect customer data.

Security measures include data encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, audit logging, multi-factor authentication, and continuous monitoring.

Security reviews and penetration tests are conducted regularly.

category: security
title: Authentication and Access Control

NimbusDesk supports email and password authentication, Single Sign-On (SSO), Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), and enterprise identity providers.

Supported SSO providers include Okta, Microsoft Entra ID, and Google Workspace.

Administrators can enforce MFA requirements for all users within their organization.

title: Reporting and Analytics

NimbusDesk provides reporting dashboards that help organizations measure support performance.

Available metrics include first response time, average resolution time, ticket volume, customer satisfaction scores, SLA compliance, and agent productivity.

Reports can be exported in CSV and PDF formats.
